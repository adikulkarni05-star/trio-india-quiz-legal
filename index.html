Privacy Policy

Trio India Quiz  ·  Effective date: [06/07/2026]

Short version: Trio India Quiz does not require an account, does not collect personal information, and does not send any data to us or to any third party. Everything the app remembers about your progress — your score, rank, achievements, and streaks — stays stored only on your own device.

1. No Account Required

Trio India Quiz does not require you to sign up, log in, or create an account of any kind to play. There is no username, password, email address, or phone number collected at any point.

2. What Information the App Stores — and Where

The app stores certain information so it can remember your progress between sessions. This information is saved only in local storage on your own device (a standard mechanism built into your phone's operating system) and is never transmitted to us, to our servers, or to any third party — because the app does not have a server or backend at all.

This locally-stored information includes:

  • Your score, rank, and XP progress
  • Achievement badges you've unlocked and when
  • Your daily login streak and Daily Challenge history
  • App preferences — sound, haptics, and light/dark theme settings

Because this data never leaves your device, we have no way to see it, access it, back it up, or recover it if you uninstall the app or switch devices. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes this information.

3. Sharing Your Results

The app includes an optional "Share Result" feature that lets you share your score or achievements as an image or text message through apps already installed on your phone (for example, WhatsApp). When you use this feature, your device's own share menu opens and you choose where that content goes. We do not receive, store, or have any visibility into what you share or who you share it with.

4. Third-Party Services

Trio India Quiz does not currently use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not display ads.

(No other third-party services are currently in use. This policy will be updated in advance of any future addition.)

5. Children's Privacy

Trio India Quiz is a general-audience trivia app and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children, since the app does not collect personal information from any user at all.

6. Permissions

The app may request standard device permissions required for its core features to work (for example, storage access to temporarily generate a shareable image file, or notification permissions if reminders are enabled). These permissions are used strictly for the on-device feature they support and are never used to collect or transmit personal data.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes — for example, if a future update adds a feature that involves data leaving your device — we will update this page and revise the effective date above accordingly.
